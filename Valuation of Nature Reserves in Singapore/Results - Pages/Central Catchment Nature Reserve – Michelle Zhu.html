WHAT ARE SOME CHARACTERISTICS OF OUR RESPONDENTS?*

REGION OF RESIDENCE

The largest proportions of respondents reside in the Central and East parts of Singapore.

COMMUTE

Majority of respondents travel to CCNR by public transportation as opposed to on foot or by private hire.

AGE

Most respondents are middle-aged, or in their 30s and 40s.

RELATIONSHIP STATUS

Percentage of respondents in a relationship is slightly greater than that of respondents who are single.

ADMISSION FEE

A larger proportion of respondents are willing to pay an admission fee to enter CCNR.

*Each chart indicates percentage and number of respondents for a particular attribute

What are the most commonly used words among respondents?

For CCNR, respondents most frequently used the words “nature”, “exercise”, “near”, “relaxing” and “walk” in their reasons for visiting the nature park.
