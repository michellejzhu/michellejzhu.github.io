WHAT ARE SOME CHARACTERISTICS OF OUR RESPONDENTS?*

REGION OF RESIDENCE

The largest proportion of respondents reside in the West part of Singapore.

COMMUTE

Largest proportion of respondents travel to LNR by private hire vehicle as opposed to on foot or by public transportation.

AGE

Most respondents are middle-aged, or in their 30s and 40s.

RELATIONSHIP STATUS

More respondents are in a relationship than are single. 

ADMISSION FEE

A larger proportion of respondents are willing to pay an admission fee to enter LNR.

*Each chart indicates percentage and number of respondents for a particular attribute.

What are the most commonly used words among respondents?

For LNR, respondents most frequently used the words “nature”, “birding”, “home”, “time” and “animals” in their reasons for visiting the nature park.
